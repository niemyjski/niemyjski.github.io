Microsoft offers four free gigs of Silverlight hosting!

December 28, 2010

1 minute read

  • Twitter

  • Facebook

  • Reddit

  • LinkedIn

  • Email

Microsoft is offering four gigs of hosting space to anyone needing a place to host there Silverlight applications. I just hope a lot of people will see the importance in the stance Microsoft is taking on this subject. So what are you waiting for? Go and get yourself some free hosting.

"Microsoft® Silverlight™ Streaming by Windows Live™ is a companion service for Silverlight that makes it easier for developers and designers to deliver and scale rich media as part of their Silverlight applications. The service offers web designers and developers a free and convenient solution for hosting and streaming cross-platform, cross-browser media experiences and rich interactive applications that run on Windows™ and Mac. Combined with the ability to create content with Microsoft® Expression Studio and other 3rd party tools, Web designers and content publishers wishing to integrate Silverlight applications into their online properties can enjoy complete control of the end user experience*” Microsoft Silverlight Streaming.
